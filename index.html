Dino Game, also known as the "Google Chrome Dinosaur Game" was originally designed to entertain users when their internet connection goes offline in the Google Chrome web browser.

In Dinosaur Game, players take control of a cute, pixelated dinosaur character and guide it through a side-scrolling desert landscape filled with obstacles such as cacti and flying pterodactyls. The objective is to keep the dinosaur running as far as possible while jumping over obstacles to avoid collision.
